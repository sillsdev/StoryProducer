Chicken and Millipede

Kakaruk na Milipit

Level 4, version 2.0

English

Chicken and Millipede were friends, but they were always competing with each other. One day they decided to play football to see who was

the best player.

Kakaruk na Milipit tupela i bin gutpela poroman stret. Tasol olgeta taim, tupela i save laik long resis namel long tupela yet. Wanpela taim, tupela i pasim tok long pilai soka long lukim husat tru em i namba wan man bilong pilai.

They went to the football field and started their game. Chicken was fast, but Millipede was faster. Chicken kicked far, but Millipede kicked further. Chicken started to feel grumpy.

Tupela i go long hap bilong pilai na i statim pilai bilong tupela. Kakaruk i ron spit, tasol Milipit i spit moa.

Kakaruk i kikim bal go longwe, tasol Milipit i kikim go longwe moa.

Kakaruk i stat long bel hat long Milipit nau.

They decided to play a penalty shoot-out. First Millipede was goal keeper. Chicken scored only one goal. Then it was the chicken’s turn to defend the goal.

Tupela i kirap na i tok bai tupela i go na traim penolti sut aut. Milipit i sanap olsem gol kipa pas. Na Kakaruk i kikim wanpela gol tasol. Bihain, em taim bilong Kakaruk long sanap olsem gol kipa.

Millipede kicked the ball and scored. Millipede dribbled the ball and scored. Millipede headed the ball and scored. Millipede scored five goals.

Milipit i kikim bal na em i go insait na em gol. Milipit i pilai wantaim bal i go na kikim i go insait na em gol. Milipit i yusim het bilong em na paitim bal i go insait na em gol. Milipit i kikim faivpela gol olgeta na olgeta i go insait.

Chicken was furious that she lost. She was a very bad loser. Millipede started laughing because his friend was making such a fuss.

Kakaruk i bel hat tru olsem em i lus. Em wanpela meri nogut taim em lus long gem.

Milipit i stat long lap long poroman bilong em, long wanem, em i bel nogut nating long dispela liklik samting.

Chicken was so angry that she opened her beak wide and swallowed the millipede.

Kakaruk i bel hat nogut tru na em i kirap na opim maus bilong em, na daunim Milipit i go insait.

As Chicken was walking home, she met Mother Millipede. Mother Millipede asked, “Have you seen my child?” Chicken didn’t say anything. Mother Millipede was worried.

Taim Kakaruk i wokabaut i go long haus, em i bungim Mama Milipit. Mama Milipit i askim, “Yu lukim pikinini bilong mi, tu?”

Kakaruk i no mekim wanpela tok, em i pasim maus tasol. Mama Milipit i wari long pikinini bilong em.

Then Mother Millipede heard a tiny voice. “Help me, Mum!” cried the voice. Mother Millipede looked around and listened carefully. The voice came from inside the chicken.

Bihain Mama Milipit i harim wanpela liklik nek. “Mama helpim mi!” dispela liklik nek i singaut olsem. Mama Milipit i lukluk raun na i putim yau gut na i wok long traim long harim. Na em i harim olsem dispela liklik nek i kam insait long bel bilong Kakaruk.

Mother Millipede shouted, “Use your special gift my child!” Millipedes can make a bad smell and a terrible taste. Chicken began to feel ill.

Mama Milipit i singaut, “Yusim pawa bilong yu!” Ol Milipit i gat wanpela kain pawa. Ol i ken rausim wanpela smel nogut na mekim teis nogut i kamap long maus sapos yu daunim ol. Kakaruk i stat long pilim sik.

Chicken burped. Then she swallowed and spat. Then she sneezed and coughed. And coughed. The millipede was disgusting!

Kakaruk i stat long kus nau.

Na em i daunim spet na spetim gen. Em i kus na kus. Na kus i go i go. Dispela Milipit i teis nogut tru!

Chicken coughed until she coughed out the millipede in her stomach. Mother Millipede and her child crawled up a tree to hide.

Kakaruk i kus, kus go inap em i trautim Milipit i kam autsait. Mama Milipit na pikinini bilong em i hariap tru i go antap long diwai na hait.

From that time, chickens and millipedes were enemies.

Long dispela taim i kam inap nau, ol kakaruk na milipit i save stap olsem birua.

Kompriensen Kwesten

1. Why did Chicken and Millipede decide to play a football game?

1. Bilong wanem na kakaruk wantaim milipit i tingting long pilai soka gem?

a) It was a nice day.

Em i gutpela de.

b) They wanted to see who was the best player.

Long lukim husat tru em i namba wan man bilong pilai.

c) They always played football together.

Ol i save pilia soka olgetata taim wantem.

Check Your Understanding

Kompriensen Kwesten

2. Why was Chicken furious after the game?

2. Bilong wanem na kakaruk i belhat bihain long gem i pinis?

a) Millipede did not play fair.

Milipit ino pilai gem stret.

b) Chicken lost.

Kakaruk i lus long pilai.

c) Millipede lost.

Milipit i lus long pilai.

Kompriensen Kwesten

3. What was Millipede’s special power?

3. Milipit i gat wanem kain spesol pawa?

a) Millipedes have a very hard shell.

Milipit i gat strongpela skin.

b) Millipedes have very sharp teeth.

Milipit i gat sapela tit.

c) Millipedes can make a terrible smell and a bad taste.

Milipit save rausim wanpela smel nogut na mekim teis nogut i kamap long maus.
