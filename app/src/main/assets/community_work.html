Community Phase

Purpose: The purpose of the COMMUNITY phase is to check that the story is meaningful and told in a clear and natural way. As the translator, you will need to meet with language speakers from the community who were not involved with the LEARN and RECORD phases. These community people will complete the COMMUNITY phase activities with you nearby.

Activities:  

  1. Listen to the entire local language translation. 

    1. Begin with the title page and tap the play button in the middle of the screen. Then swipe to the next page, tap play, and listen. 

    2. Listen to the entire story one page at a time.  

  2. Suggest improvements. Listen to the story again, page by page. 

  1. Tell the translator about anything that does not sound right.  

  2. Suggest how to make that part of the story sound better.  

  3. Record audio comments for each page by tapping the mic button on the bottom of the screen.  Tap the square to stop the audio recording.

  1. Suggest improvements for things such as:

  1. Different words used for the same item, for example: sack and bag 

  2. Words or names that are pronounced differently in the same story

  3. Words or phrases that are borrowed from another language

  4. Words or phrases that sound out of order (bad grammar)

  5. Words or phrases where the meaning is unclear or where there is more than one meaning possible

  6. Very formal or old language that would sound more natural with an idiom 

  7. Suggest a different photo or picture from the story for the title page

  8. Suggest a different title for the story

Notes: 

  • This work space for the community intentionally does not include the source text for each slide.  The community or peer review should focus not on translating, but rather on evaluating and editing the provided translation to be natural and clear in the language.

  • You can record as many comments as necessary for each page. If you want to inform the accuracy checker/consultant about an issue, record it in a language s/he speaks.

  • If more than four comments are recorded, you will need to scroll down the screen to access them. To scroll within the phase without swiping to another phase, press and scroll (move your finger up or down) or scroll while pressing your finger. 

  • You can rename a comment by pressing on the “comment name” to bring up the phone keyboard. Type a new name. 

  • Play or delete any comment as desired. Delete a comment once it has been resolved.
