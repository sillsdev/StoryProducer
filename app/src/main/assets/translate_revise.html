Record Phase

Purpose: The purpose of the RECORD phase is to record the story one page at a time. Also, revisions to the translation draft will be recorded here during and after the Community and Accuracy checks.  

Activities:

  1. Record the story

    1. Tap the play button in the picture to listen to the audio narration for a page. (The audio narration normally is the same as the written text.)  

    2. At the bottom of the screen, tap the microphone button and record that part of the story in your language. Record again if needed.

    3. Tap the triangle playback button to listen to your recording. Can you hear it clearly? Is the translation natural? If not, record again.

  2. Add a photo to contextualize the title page. 

    1. Tap the camera icon at the top left of the title page. 

    2. Turn your phone sideways to the landscape position and take a photo of something in your community that helps identify your language group. This photo will be the background to the title. The photo can also be a design or other artwork. 

  3. Choose a good title for the story. Type the title words in your language. Record the title to match the text.

  4. Swipe up to move to the COMMUNITY phase or use the drop down menu on the top of the screen to tap to another phase. 

Notes:

  • Swipe right and left to move from page to page, from picture to picture.

  • Pages are numbered in the lower right corner of the picture.

  • After the last page, swipe right again to return to the beginning of the story.

  • Sometimes there are words in square brackets [ ] to help you with the translation. You can decide if you want to translate those words. 

  • Translate the entire text or meaning for the slide in one recording. You cannot pause and resume your recording in the RECORD phase.

  • Record as many times as needed, until you are satisfied.

  • The app will automatically save all audio translation drafts. The most recent (the last) translation recording will always be the selected default, unless you manually choose another draft from the play list.

  • You can review all your drafts by tapping the List Play icon on the lower right of the screen. You can play, delete, rename (press and hold, then type), and select drafts to be the default from the list window.
