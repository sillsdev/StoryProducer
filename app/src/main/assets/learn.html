Learn Phase

Purpose: The purpose of the LEARN phase is to learn the story well and to practice telling it in your language. This is also the place to decide how to translate difficult words and names. The main person doing this work will be referred to as the “translator” in this document.

Activities: 

  1. Play the story as many times as necessary to learn the story well.

  2. Practice telling the story in your language until you can tell the story well. Try to include all the information in the story. Swipe up or use the drop down menu on the top of the screen to move to the RECORD phase. 

Notes: 

  • Mute or reduce the volume on your phone if you want to only watch the pictures as you practice retelling the story.

  • Decide how to render difficult words and names.  

    • If there is an existing local language Scripture translation or Bible translation team, the same key terms and names should be used so there is consistency in how terms and names are translated in the stories.

    • If it has not been translated, discuss the options with others. Do you have a dictionary that would help you decide?
